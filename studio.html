The Creative Process

How every piece comes to life.

01
✏️

Sketch & Concept

Every ADW piece starts as a hand-drawn sketch. We explore silhouettes, proportions, and cultural motifs before a single pattern is cut. Heritage guides every line.

02
🎨

Colour & Material

Kente-inspired palettes meet contemporary fabric research. Each colourway is built to honour traditional dye traditions while performing in modern streetwear contexts.

03
🧵

Sampling & Refine

Samples are produced, tested, and refined with community feedback. We iterate until every stitch, every graphic, every detail is exactly as it should be.

Beyond Clothing

Ancestral
Fragrance

The ADW fragrance line is rooted in the same philosophy as our garments — cultural memory made tangible. Each scent is a journey through African botanical traditions, from the earthy warmth of shea to the clean brightness of citrus groves at dawn. The bottle designs are architectural studies in African form.
