Ancestral

A.D.W is a contemporary African fashion and lifestyle brand that bridges generations — drawing from the visual language of kente, ankara, and adinkra while crafting pieces for tomorrow's wardrobe. Our garments, accessories, and fragrances are objects of cultural memory, made to be worn and remembered.
